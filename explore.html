About CribForest

A real-estate explorer rooted in location intelligence — built for Springfield, Missouri. Find a crib that fits the life you're actually living.

Listing sites tell you what a home is. CribForest tells you where it puts you. Every property is scored on drive time and distance to 11 amenity categories — fire, police, schools at four levels, hospitals, urgent care, public health, nursing homes, and trailheads — using real OSRM-routed network distances on the OpenStreetMap road network.

How match scoring works

For each amenity you mark important, we check whether the home's drive time falls under your maximum. The match score is the share of your priorities the home satisfies. Homes that hit every priority earn a perfect match and surface to the top.

Data sources

  • 2,000 Springfield properties with parcel-level geometry (UTM 15N, reprojected to WGS84)
  • American Community Survey 2018–2023 (income, race, education, occupancy)
  • Routed drive times via OpenStreetMap road network
  • FEMA flood intersections & Springfield NSA boundaries
